Core Guide

Common Head-to-Head Matchups

RPM Blast vs Hyper-G

Two modern spin favorites

RPM Blast is often described as a little smoother and more controlled, while Hyper-G is often associated with a slightly firmer, more grabby spin response. Players often compare them when choosing a shaped performance poly.

ALU Power vs 4G

Feel vs stability

ALU Power is often chosen for its livelier feel and touch, while 4G is usually associated with a firmer, more locked-in response and stronger tension stability over time.

X-One Biphase vs Wilson NXT

Premium multi matchup

Players often compare these when they want comfort and power without going to natural gut. The decision usually comes down to preferred feel, crispness, and pocketing.

Poly Tour Pro vs Lynx Tour

Smoother poly vs firmer spin option

Poly Tour Pro is often seen as the friendlier, smoother all-around poly, while Lynx Tour tends to appeal to players who want a more direct and controlled response.

Natural Gut vs Multifilament

Premium comfort paths

This is a common comparison for players prioritizing feel and arm comfort. Gut is usually the premium benchmark, while multifilament often serves as the more accessible practical alternative.

Round Poly vs Shaped Poly

Predictability vs extra bite

Players often compare these categories more than exact strings. Round poly usually feels smoother and cleaner, while shaped poly is often chosen for a sharper spin-oriented response.
