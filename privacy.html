Privacy Summary

What data is used

Browsing

Basic visit analytics

Tennis String Lab may collect lightweight visit statistics so the site owner can understand traffic and page usage. This is used for product insight, not for account creation.

Local Browser Storage

Saved images and custom pro notes

If you use the image library or custom pro-player tools, that information can be stored locally in your browser on your device so the planner can reuse it later on that same device.

External Links

Third-party sites

Brand pages, retailer links, and other external destinations have their own privacy policies. Tennis String Lab does not control how those third parties handle your data.
