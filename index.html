Suggested starting point

Solinco Hyper-G 17 @ 48-50 lbs

High-spin poly recommendation for players who want heavier ball shape and a lower launch window without giving away control.

Why this is a fit

  • Improves bite on crosscourt forehands and kick serves.
  • Keeps bigger cuts from sailing when you accelerate harder.
  • Still gives a clean benchmark tension range to adjust from.

Try this if you want more comfort

  • Start 2 lbs lower if you want easier pocketing and net clearance.
  • Shift to a softer co-poly if your elbow or shoulder gets tender.
  • Compare against one round poly before going too shaped too fast.

Start with the problem

When a setup is wrong, it usually shows up in three ways.

You do not need more specs first. You need a better starting diagnosis.

Too much power

Balls launch long, your swing speed drops, and you stop trusting your targets.

No spin or shape

You work hard for net clearance and kick, but the ball still flies too flat.

Arm discomfort

The setup feels harsh, dead, or punishing over a full hitting session.
